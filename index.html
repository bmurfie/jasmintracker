20-Week Hyper-Anabolic Protocol

Mission Objectives: Recomposition & Regrowth

This protocol is engineered to aggressively leverage myonuclei retention (muscle memory) over 20 weeks, converting stored energy into high-quality muscle fiber.

Critical Risks & Monitoring

Zero side effects are not guaranteed.

  • CVS Load: High volume and compound usage demand constant blood pressure/lipid monitoring.
  • Tissue Integrity: Tendons/joints must be protected. High-rep warm-ups and BPC-157 are mandatory.
  • Bloodwork: Critical monitoring every 4–6 weeks to guide protocol adjustments.

Performance Goals

  • Primary Objective: Achieve significant body recomposition (aggressive fat loss with maximal muscle gain).
  • Weakness Eradication: Systematically eliminate the arm weakness via high-frequency training.
  • Initial Target: Transition from 205 lb to a lean, conditioned 215–225 lb by Week 20.

Protocol Phase Architecture

  • Phase 1 (Weeks 1–4): High volume, lower intensity, tissue stabilization.
  • Phase 2 (Weeks 5–12): Increased load, PPL ×2, Nandrolone initiated for joint support.
  • Phase 3 (Weeks 13–20): Body-part split, RPE 9–10, maximal caloric/compound saturation.

Training Protocol Overload

Primary Focus: Progressive Overload & High-Frequency Arms

Phase 1: Re-Awakening

Weeks 1–4

  • Frequency: 6 days on / 1 day off
  • Intensity: RPE 7 (≈3 reps in reserve)
  • Reps: 12–20 (joint conditioning / capillary density)
  • Split: Full-body / Upper–Lower hybrid

Phase 2: Intensity Drive

Weeks 5–12

  • Frequency: 6 days on / 1 day off
  • Intensity: RPE 8–9 (1–2 reps in reserve)
  • Reps: 8–12 (primary hypertrophy)
  • Split: PPL ×2
  • Technique: Introduce BFR for arms

Phase 3: Peak Overload

Weeks 13–20

  • Frequency: 5 days on / 2 days off
  • Intensity: RPE 9–10 (final sets to failure)
  • Reps: 6–12 (strength & size)
  • Split: Body-part split (prioritize recovery)
  • Technique: Giant sets, forced reps, heavy negatives

Sample Workout: Phase 2 — Pull Day

  • Heavy T-Bar Row (4 × 8–10)
  • Chest-Supported Row (4 × 10–12)
  • Weighted Chins (4 × 8–12)
  • Face Pulls (3 × 15)
  • Biceps: 1 set of 100-rep Cable Curls (rest-pause)

Nutrition & Recovery Strategy

Precision Carb Cycling for Hypertrophy & Fat Loss

Macro Constants

  • Protein: 308 g minimum (≈1.5 g/lb)
  • Sleep: 9 hours
  • Meal Prep: Non-negotiable for adherence

Supplementation

  • Creatine: 5 g daily
  • Intra-Workout: EAAs + fast carbs (high days)
  • Post-Workout: Protein isolate + 75–100 g HGC (high days)
  • Omega-3s: 4–6 g EPA/DHA daily
